About Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV)

Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), also known as Devi Ahilya University, is a prominent public university located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. Established in 1964, the university was initially known as the University of Indore and later renamed in honor of the legendary queen of the Maratha Empire, Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar, known for her contributions to society, art, and architecture. DAVV offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across disciplines like science, arts, commerce, law, management, engineering, education, and social sciences. The university has numerous academic departments and affiliated colleges that cater to different fields of study, including technology, health sciences, and professional courses.
DAVV is accredited with an ‘A+’ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). It consistently ranks among the top universities in India in various national rankings like the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). There are over 200 colleges affiliated with Devi Ahilya University, offering diverse courses in streams like medical, engineering, commerce, and arts. The university provides modern amenities like libraries, sports complexes, hostels, and research labs. It has a vibrant student community with cultural events, fests, and extracurricular activities happening throughout the academic year. DAVV has collaborations with national and international institutions, facilitating academic exchange programs, research partnerships, and joint projects. Devi Ahilya University continues to be a leading educational institution in central India, fostering academic excellence and contributing to research and development across multiple fields. .

About International Institute of Professional Studies (IIPS)

The International Institute of Professional Studies (IIPS) is a renowned academic institution under Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), Indore. Established in 1992, IIPS is a pioneer institute to provide a new dimension to professional education. IIPS is a realm where education meets innovation, and dreams take flight. The Institute has been providing exceptional opportunities to the students for the past 32 years. IIPS is known for its integrated and professional courses in the fields of management, computer science, and commerce. The institute has gained a reputation as a center of excellence, particularly for offering five-year integrated programs, which are designed to provide students with comprehensive education and professional training. IIPS stands as a paragon of academic and industry excellence and a beacon of innovation in professional studies. The Institute is located in the sprawling Takshashila campus of the University.
IIPS is ranked among the top institutes in central India for professional studies and has gained recognition in both national and international academic circles. Since its inception, the Institute has been dedicated to fostering an environment where scholarly rigor, professional development, and personal growth converge harmoniously. The institute offers a diverse range of programs, meticulously designed to address the evolving demands of the professional world. This martinet institute offers integrated post-graduate courses in the fields of computer and management which students join directly after schooling. In addition to that institute serves specialized courses in commerce, advertisement, tourism and public relations management as well. The institute provides a rigorous, intellectually challenging curriculum with flexibility to allow students to tailor their education to meet their professional and personal interests and goals. It provides a collaborative learning environment that weaves technology and management into everyday life. With the quality in the content, scope and professionalism of its cross-disciplinary programs, IIPS is one of the best centers of technical and management education in central India.
IIPS boasts modern infrastructure with well-equipped classrooms, computer labs, a library, seminar halls, and internet access to support the educational needs of students. The institute provides a conducive environment for learning, with a focus on blending theoretical knowledge with practical applications through workshops, seminars, and projects. The institute has a highly qualified faculty, most of whom hold doctorates and are actively involved in research and consultancy. IIPS is a research center for Faculty of Management and Faculty of Engineering Sciences. Students are encouraged to participate in research projects, which fosters a spirit of inquiry and innovation among them.
IIPS has strong ties with industry and regularly organizes internships, guest lectures, and industrial visits to provide students with real-world exposure. The placement cell at IIPS is active, ensuring that students receive ample placement opportunities with top companies in India and abroad. Companies from sectors like IT, banking, FMCG, consultancy, and others regularly recruit from the institute. IIPS has a vibrant student culture, with various clubs and societies promoting extracurricular activities such as debates, cultural fests, and sports events. IIPS is consistently ranked among the top institutions in central India for professional education. It is known for its student-centric teaching methodology, industry-integrated curriculum, and focus on overall personality development. The IIPS remains a top choice for students aspiring to build careers in management, information technology, and commerce, with a strong focus on professional and integrated learning.
